Jerry Lawson

1940 - 2011

If you have ever played a video game chances are Jerry Lawson has been an influence on your life. Jerry and his team at FairChild Semiconductors designed the first cartiage base home video gaming system. This is the system that gave rise to the Atari 2600 , the Nintendo Entertainment System and so on. Jerry also create the coin-op arcade game Demolition Derby. Thanks to him video games had an easy and cost effective way to come home with us.
